Real-time Feedback

Ongoing or Instantaneous Updates

Real-time Feedback

  • Feedback mechanisms already commonly used by instructors:
    • Turning Point, Socrative, Kahoot
    • Asking questions
      • Raised hands
      • Verbal answer
    • Looking at your students' faces

Real-time Feedback

  • Serves multiple purposes:
    • Grab students' attention
    • Encourage questions
    • Help the instructor adjust their pace/delivery
